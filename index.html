Tapioca pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of tapioca flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • A slice of cheese

Directions

  1. Stirr the egg in a bowl
  2. Add the tapioca and salt and mix it
  3. Pour the mix into a pan
  4. Add the cheese
  5. After 1 minute, flip the pancake and leave it for another 30s

Now you have your tapioca pancake!
